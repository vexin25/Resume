Kyoshyou Lifestyle Products

E-Commerce Platform

The name "今日醬油" is derived from Japanese and is a combination of the three creators' names as a play on words. We are dedicated to making our platform as essential as soy sauce in daily life—adding a splash of color to everyday routines.

Kyoshyou Lifestyle Product is a P2P e-commerce platform that integrates social interaction. Users can log in as either buyers or sellers. Buyers can leave comments under product listings and share their experiences with others by posting on the platform. Sellers, on the other hand, can manage product inventory through the backend and analyze sales and customer data using visualized charts.

In addition, whenever users interact through purchases, comments, or posts, the platform sends real-time notifications to keep users informed and engaged.

  • Develop the UI/UX with HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Built a shopping cart and order summary using Vue.js.
  • Visualized sales data in the seller dashboard using D3.js.
  • Applied Moment.js to handle time settings
